Analyze my Monobank account data from the last 30 days and create a comprehensive financial dashboard.

**Create an interactive HTML dashboard with:**

**1. Account Overview Section:**
- Display all accounts with their types, balances, and credit limits
- Show currency breakdown across accounts
- List all cards (masked PANs) and their IBANs
- Highlight cashback types and total cashback earned in the last 30 days

**2. Savings Goals Tracking:**
- Visual progress bars for each jar
- Show title, description, current balance, and goal
- Calculate total savings and completion percentages
- Provide motivation insights on savings progress

**3. Transaction Analytics (Last 30 Days):**
- **Spending patterns**: weekday vs weekend, time of day distribution
- **Category analysis**: MCC-based breakdown with percentages and visual charts
- **Merchant insights**: top merchants by frequency and total amount
- **Cashback analysis**: total earned, best cashback merchants
- **Income vs expenses**: weekly breakdown with trends
- **Balance trends**: daily balance evolution chart over the period

**4. Financial Insights (Based on 30-Day Period):**
- Budget health score based on income/expense ratio
- Unusual transactions or spending spikes detection
- Week-over-week comparison and trends
- Average daily spending calculation
- Projected monthly spending (extrapolated from 30 days)
- Recommendations for budget optimization
- Warnings about approaching credit limits

**5. Currency Information:**
- Current exchange rates for account currencies
- Rate comparison (buy vs sell)
- Multi-currency account impact analysis

**6. Visualizations:**
- Interactive charts for spending by category (pie/donut chart)
- Timeline of daily spending and income (last 30 days)
- Balance trend line chart
- Top 10 merchants bar chart
- Savings progress visual indicators
- Weekly comparison charts

**Design Requirements:**
- Clean, modern, responsive design
- Interactive charts (hover for details, clickable legends)
- Color-coded insights (green for income, red for expenses, blue for savings)
- Clear data tables for recent transactions
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Dark mode friendly color scheme
- Display date range prominently (last 30 days)

Provide actionable financial insights, budget recommendations, and spending warnings based on the 30-day analysis period.
